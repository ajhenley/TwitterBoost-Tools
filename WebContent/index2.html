Welcome

Twitter Boost Tools is a web application to boost your Twitter account/follower engagement. For more information and to see the source code of the project, checkout the GitHub repository. Created by @warrencrasta.

Tools:

  1. Unfollow users who don't follow you back (with an option to save a group of people from being unfollowed).
  2. Follow users based on a keyword they tweeted (can follow a maximum of 1000 people every 24 hours).
  3. Follow users based on a keyword in their bio (can follow a maximum of 1000 people every 24 hours).
  4. Favorite tweets based on a keyword (can favorite a maximum of 1000 tweets every 24 hours).
  5. Unfavorite all of your tweets.
